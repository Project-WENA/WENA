# How to Post a New Blog Article on the WENA Website
*(No coding needed — takes about 5 minutes)*

---

## Step 1: Open the Admin Panel
Go to: **yourwebsite.com/admin**
(Replace "yourwebsite.com" with your actual site link)

## Step 2: Log In
Click **"Login with GitHub"** (or "Login" if using Netlify Identity) and sign in with the account set up for you.

## Step 3: Click "New Blog Articles"
You'll see a button on the left side. Click it to start a new post.

## Step 4: Fill in the Form
- **Title** — the headline of your article
- **Cover Image** — click to upload a photo (optional but recommended)
- **Short Summary** — 1-2 lines that show up on the blog listing page
- **Publish Date** — pick today's date (or a future date if you want to schedule it)
- **Article Content** — write your article here. You can bold text, add headings, insert images, and add links using the toolbar at the top of the box.

## Step 5: Publish
Click the **"Publish"** button in the top-right corner, then confirm.

That's it! Your article will appear on the **Blog** page of the website within about a minute — no developer needed.

---

## Editing or Deleting an Article
1. Go back to **yourwebsite.com/admin**
2. Click on the article you want to change
3. Edit the content and click **Publish** again — or click the trash icon to delete it

## Comments
Every article automatically has a comments section at the bottom (powered by Disqus). You don't need to do anything — it just works. If you ever want to moderate or delete a comment, you can log in at **disqus.com** with the account that was set up for the site.

## If Something Looks Wrong
- Refresh the page — sometimes it just needs a moment to update.
- If an article isn't showing up, double check you clicked **Publish** (not just Save).
- For anything else, reach out to your developer.
